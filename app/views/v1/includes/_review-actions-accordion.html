Call the claimant

Check if the claimant has already told us about any missing evidence.

If they have not told us about any missing evidence or you need to discuss permitted expenses call the claimant and discuss the review.

Explain to the claimant:

• that they can only claim for expenses that they have evidence for

• that they will need to provide the missing evidence if they have it

• that if they do not have the missing evidence or have claimed for expenses that are not permitted they will need to complete a new ‘Report your income and expenses’ to-do

If you contact the claimant

• If there is evidence missing and the claimant can provide it, you should follow the instructions in ‘request missing evidence’.

• If they do not have the missing evidence or they have claimed expenses that are not permitted you should follow the instructions in ‘Request a new ‘Report your income and expenses’ to-do.
