[List the specific evidence you need]

Suspend the claim

Actions

To suspend a claim:

1. Select ’Claim actions’ from the left hand menu.
2. Select ’Suspension’ from the options presented.
3.Suspend the claimant’s account and select ’Claimant has not supplied evidence’.

Close the claim

1. Remove yourself as supporting on the claim.
2. Close the claim by selecting ‘Claim actions’ from the left hand menu.
3. Select ‘Close claim’ and complete the checks.
4. Select the appropriate reason for closing the claim.

If the claimant fails to provide evidence or complete a to-do use ‘Failed to provide evidence’.

If the claimant asks us to close the claim use ‘Withdrew their claim’
