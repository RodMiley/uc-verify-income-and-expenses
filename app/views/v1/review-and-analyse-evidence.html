What you need to do

{% if data['reviewEvidence']=='Suspended the claim (Awaiting a report your income and expenses to do)' %}

Check if the claimant has completed the ‘Report your income and expenses’ to-do.

If the claimant has not completed the requested to-do follow the instructions in ‘Close the claim’.

If they have completed the to-do check if all the expenses are permitted and match what was agreed.

If the expenses are not permitted or do not match what was agreed ‘Close the claim’.

If all the expenses are permitted and the evidence matches follow the instructions in ‘lift the suspension’ and complete this task.

{% elif data['reviewEvidence']=='Suspended the claim (Awaiting missing evidence)' %}

1. Check if the claimant has completed the requested to-do and provided the missing evidence requested.

If the claimant has not completed the requested to-do follow the instructions in ‘Close the claim’.

If they have completed the to-do follow the instructions in ‘lift the suspension’.

After you lift the suspension check that the expenses are all permitted and the evidence matches what was agreed.

If all the expenses are permitted and the evidence matches complete this task.

2. If the evidence does not match what was agreed or they have reported expenses that are not permitted follow the instructions in ‘Create a new ‘Report your income and expenses’.

{% elif data['reviewEvidence']=='Awaiting a new ‘Report your income and expenses to do’' %}

Check if the claimant has completed the ‘Report your income and expenses’ to-do.

If the claimant has not completed the requested to-do follow the instructions in ‘Suspend the claim’.

If they have completed the to-do check if all the expenses are permitted and match what was agreed. If all the expenses are permitted and the evidence matches complete this task.

If the to-do does not match what was agreed you can:

• ‘Suspend the claim’ and ‘Create a new ‘Report your income and expenses’

• ‘Suspend the claim’ and ‘request missing evidence’ if you feel it is appropriate

{% elif data['reviewEvidence']=='Awaiting missing evidence' %}

Check if the claimant has completed the requested to-do and provided the missing evidence requested.

If the claimant has not completed the requested to-do or provided the missing evidence follow the instructions in ‘Suspend the claim’.

if they have completed the to-do you need to decide whether the evidence provided matches what was agreed.

If the evidence matches what was agreed, complete this task.

You can request missing evidence again if you feel it is appropriate. Or you can ‘Create a new ‘Report your income and expenses’ to-do’.

{% elif data['reviewEvidence']=='Referred to team leader' %}

1. Read the agent notes to understand why the review has been referred to a team leader.

2. Follow the instructions in ‘Call the claimant’.

{% else %}

1. Review the reported income and expenses.

2. Check the guidance below to make sure you know:

• whether the expenses are permitted
• what you need to analyse and consider when reviewing the reported income and expenses

What you need to do after you have reviewed the evidence

1. You need to decide whether the reported income and expenses are all permitted and match the evidence provided.

If the evidence matches and the expenses are all permitted, complete this task.

2. Follow the instructions in referring the review to a Team leader if any of the following applies:

• using the home for business

• business costs if working from home

• living at their place of business

• simplified vs actual costs, including vehicle expenses

• anything else that you feel needs further investigation

If you do not need to refer the review to a Team leader follow the instructions in ‘Call the claimant’.
