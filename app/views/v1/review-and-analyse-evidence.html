Next steps

{% if data['reviewEvidence']=='Suspended the claim' %}

After suspending the claim

If the claimant has provided the evidence we requested or completed the to-do you should lift the suspension.

You should then review the evidence and decide whether the reported income and expenses match what was agreed. If the evidence matches, complete this task.

If the missing evidence does not match, you need to Request a new ‘Report your income and expenses’ to-do.

If the ‘Report your income and expenses’ to-do does not match what was agreed you need to close the claim.

{% elif data['reviewEvidence']=='Awaiting missing evidence' or data['reviewEvidence']=='Awaiting a new ‘Report your income and expenses to do’' %}

{% if data['reviewEvidence']=='Awaiting missing evidence' %} After requesting missing evidence {% else %} After creating a new 'Report your income and expenses' to-do {% endif %}

If you receive what we requested, you need to decide whether the reported income and expenses match what was agreed.

If the evidence matches what was agreed, complete this task.

Suspend the claim if the claimant has not:

• provided the missing evidence requested
• completed the requested to-do

You can request missing evidence again if you feel it is appropriate.

{% elif data['reviewEvidence']=='Referred to team leader' %}

Call the claimant

If the evidence does not match or the claimant has claimed expenses that are not permitted you should call the claimant to discuss the review.

After the call

• If their is missing evidence and the claimant can provide it create a new upload to-do.

• If their is missing evidence and the claimant cannot provide or their are none permitted expenses create a new 'Report your income and expenses to-do'.

Do not create a new 'Report your income and expenses' to-do if you have requested more evidence.

{% else %}

Consider referring to a Team leader

You need to decide whether the reported income and expenses are all permitted and match the evidence provided.

If the evidence matches, complete this task.

If the evidence does not match, refer the review to a team leader if any of the following applies:

• using the home for business

• business costs if working from home

• living at their place of business

• simplified vs actual costs, including vehicle expenses

• anything else that you feel needs further investigation

If you need to refer this case to your team leader, follow the refer to team leader instructions.

Call the claimant

If the evidence does not match or the claimant has claimed expenses that are not permitted you should call the claimant to discuss the review.

After the call

• If their is missing evidence and the claimant can provide it create a new upload to-do.

• If their is missing evidence and the claimant cannot provide or their are none permitted expenses create a new 'Report your income and expenses to-do'.

Do not create a new 'Report your income and expenses' to-do if you have requested more evidence.

{% endif %}
